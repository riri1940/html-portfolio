Must Watch movies According to Rita

The Top Five... Here They are!

Mufasa

Whoever thought of creating a story surrounding Mufasa's past and his connection with Scar did a great job. Thumbs up to the producers. It was worth every minute

Squid Game

If you are a lover of action movies, then this one's for you.

Woman King

The strength and hidden potentials of the African woman portrayed in its fullness. A must watch!

The Queen's Umbrella

Set in South Korea. Very engaging, suspense packed. K-drama lovers, this one's particularly for you.

24

Jack Bauer is the right guy that keeps you glued to the screen for 24 hours. I'll give it a 5-star.
